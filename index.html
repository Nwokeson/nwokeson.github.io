Research Questions

1. How can organizational leaders assess and develop their own readiness to lead AI-driven transformation?
2. What HR strategies and upskilling frameworks are most effective in preparing workforces for AI adoption?
3. How does organizational culture influence successful AI adoption, and what change management practices mitigate resistance?
4. What governance structures and ethical guidelines ensure AI systems are deployed safely and equitably?
